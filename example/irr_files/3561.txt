remarks:      |   priority one incident report at
remarks:      |   http://www.security.savvis.net (Report an Incident)
remarks:      |   email: security@savvis.net
remarks:      |
remarks:      |   Once the attack has been mitigated, the customer
remarks:      |   will be responsible for removing the prefix from
remarks:      |   being advertised.
remarks:      |

remarks:      |   customers who errantly advertise prefixes with the
